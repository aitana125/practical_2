3. Learning Outcomes

The student will be able to understand and apply all their knowledge from the specified contents in real-life problems to the degree that the material has been taught. They will be able to apply their methods of organisation and team working too outside of the subject for the rest of their degree.

This is a key subject in the study program. It assures the student is ready to tackle the majority of the computer engineering fields it introduces the student to, and provides a solid foundation for them and their future careers.
