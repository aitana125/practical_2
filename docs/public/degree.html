Main

I am studying a double degree in Mathematics and Computer Engineering.

Conocer la naturaleza, métodos y fines de los distintos campos de las matemáticas y la informática, y reconocer la presencia de la matemática subyacente en la Naturaleza, en la Ciencia, y en la Tecnología

My classes for the first four years of the course are largely dedicated to the mathematics aspect of the double degree, whilst still being required for 2 mandatory computer science classes to be taken in place of the usual optional classes single math majors have. In my fourth year I will present my TFG for the mathematics side of the degree and in my last and fifth year the classes will be entirely dedicated to the Computer Science degree, with its respective TFG needed to be presented at the end of the academic year.

To explore the different subjects my program entails, use the DEGREE button from the navegation bar that is now a dropdown menu. You will see the subjects split between the five years of the degree, as well as an extra tab dedicated to the first-year subject Fundamentals of Computer Engineering.

All the subject information has been taken from the universities public 2024/2025 study plan.
