A little about me...

My full name is Aitana Imaz Chuquimarca, and I am 18 years old. Currently, I study a double degree of Mathematics and Computer Engineering in Francisco de Vitoria University. I was born in Madrid, Spain and I have lived here my whole life. Some of my hobbies include playing the guitar, going for a run, and spending time with my loved ones!

I wanted to study Computer Engineering alongside Mathematics because I have always been interested in the study of software and programming, and how this knowledge helps computer engineers be able to build incredible things, from microprocessors to control systems.

My hope is to be able to leave this world better than when I came into it, and I think studying Computer Science will allow me the opportunity to accomplish that.

While I am still a little bit unsure of what I want my occupation to be in the future, high employability in Spain for jobs with mathematics, statistics and data science is an incentive for me to look more into those fields in the near future.
